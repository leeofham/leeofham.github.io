About me

I'm Liam, a Junior Software Engineer based in London.

I have always had a passion for learning how to make new things and the problem solving that arises on the journey. That's why I got an Advanced Level Apprenticeship in Engineering and started working in electrical, electronic and mechanical engineering.

Currently I am looking for a change in job industry and I have just recently completed a Software Engineering Immersive course at General Assembly, London. The course has got me excited and better prepared for my first job as a Software Engineer.

When I am not coding, I run an online gaming community. This involves hosting events multiple times a week and working and leading a team of 12 people from all across Europe, Middle East and Asia. I also like to keep my hands busy by building and upgrading some of my guitars, as well as playing them.
