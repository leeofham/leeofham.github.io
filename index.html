About me

I'm Liam, a Junior Software Engineer based in London.

I have always had a passion for learning how to make new things and the problem solving that arises on the journey. That's why I got an Advanced Level Apprenticeship in Engineering and started working in electrical, electronic and mechanical engineering.

I also like to keep my hands busy by building and upgrading some of my guitars, as well as playing them.
